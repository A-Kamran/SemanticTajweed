The Semantic Tajweed Ontology: Description back to ToC

The SemanticTajweed ontology is developed to formalize and preserve the rules of Quranic recitation (Tajweed), focusing on phonetic, articulation, and linguistic principles embedded in the Quranic script. This ontology serves as a structured vocabulary to model Quranic letters, diacritics, articulation points, and Tajweed rules, enabling automated reasoning over recitation correctness and phonological patterns.
